About Curova Pharma

Curova Pharma is WHO-GMP and ISO 9001:2015 certified. Established in 2002 with over 22 years of expertise, we combine research-driven innovation and strict quality controls to manufacture tablets, capsules, syrups, injections, and herbal medicines for hospitals and distributors.

Certifications & Recognition: WHO-GMP, ISO 9001:2015, ISO 14001:2018, FDA registered. National Excellence in Pharmaceutical Manufacturing (2022).

Team: 250+ professionals including pharmacists, QA scientists, and regulatory specialists.
